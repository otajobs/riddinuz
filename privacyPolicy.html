Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 25, 2026

Welcome to Kashi App (“we,” “our,” or “us”). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our AI-powered voice expense tracking mobile application (the “App”).

1. Information We Collect

a. Information You Provide

  • Voice recordings analyzed to create expense entries
  • Expense data (amounts, categories, descriptions, dates)
  • Account information (such as email address if you create an account)
  • Customer support communications

b. Automatically Collected Information

  • Device information (device type, OS version)
  • App usage data and analytics
  • Crash logs and performance data

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Convert voice input into expense records using AI processing
  • Store and organize your financial data
  • Improve app functionality and performance
  • Provide customer support
  • Comply with legal obligations

3. Voice Data Processing

Voice recordings may be processed using secure third-party AI services to convert speech into text. We do not sell or share your voice recordings for advertising purposes. Voice data is only used to provide the core functionality of the App.

4. Data Storage and Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information. Data may be stored on secure cloud servers. While we strive to protect your data, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information with:

  • Service providers who assist in app functionality (such as cloud hosting or analytics)
  • Legal authorities when required by law

6. Data Retention

We retain your data only as long as necessary to provide services or comply with legal obligations. You may request deletion of your data at any time.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Delete your data
  • Withdraw consent where applicable

8. Age restriction

The App is not intended for people under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

9. Third-Party Services

The App may use third-party services (such as analytics or AI processing providers). These services have their own privacy policies governing their data handling practices.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted within the App or on our website with an updated “Last Updated” date.
